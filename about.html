My Engineering Profile

Hello, my name is Caden Whalen, and I am a rising 2nd year student at Georgia Tech. I am currently maintaining a 4.0 GPA while pursuing bachelor's degrees in both Aerospace and Electrical Engineering. I am passionate about the field of avionics integration, with a strong foundation in technical problem-solving, communication, and collaborative design. I genuinely enjoy solving complex problems and leading innovative projects with others across technical teams.

Outside of class, I have spent my time founding the Georgia Tech Orbital Initiative. Coming into Georgia Tech, I was surprised to see that there were no satellite-focused clubs available for undergraduate students to join. Over the past six months, my friends and I have been brainstorming, designing, and creating foundational prototypes while also preparing to be officially recognized in Fall 2026. More specifically, this student organization aims to create specialized microsatellite technology with the goal of providing and testing cost-effective, open-source design solutions.

As for the future, I hope to continue to succeed academically during my time at Georgia Tech and further develop GTOI into a unique multidisciplinary opportunity for students in years to come. I am currently on track to graduate in Spring 2029, and I hope to be working in the field of avionics integration soon afterwards. After 1-2 years in the workforce, I plan to further my career experience and serve my country by applying to the Air Force Reserve.
